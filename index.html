Our Story

Every cake starts with someone we care about

Shreya Bakery began on a simple promise: a cake should taste as good as it looks, and it should arrive on time, every time. From our kitchen at Vijaya Chowk, we bake cream cakes, breads and pastries fresh each morning — nothing sits in a freezer waiting for an order.

What makes us different isn't a secret recipe. It's that we treat a two-year-old's first birthday cake with the same care as a wedding centerpiece. Look through our gallery below — every photo is a cake we actually made, for a real family in Gaindakot.
